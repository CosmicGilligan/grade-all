5.1.2 War in Europe: Olumayowa Oduntan

1. Hitler spoke the narrative that Jews had betrayed Germany and also spread the idea that Germans were at the top of the racial hierarchy. This antisemitism spread by Hitler caused the Germans to unite together.

2. We, as Americans, are not immune to this appeal. Once the leaders of the nation determine that a race or country is the enemy, we can develop hate or prejudice towards them and fall into the same cycle displayed during WWII. A recent example would be the global pandemic COVID-19, in which the world was trying to find someone to blame for the virus, and people from Asia were targeted.
